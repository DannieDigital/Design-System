Footer

The footer reflect when the website was updated and provide links to designer social media website .

Example:

Last updated February 2024.
Linkedin Instagram

Code:



<button type="button" class="btn btn-outline-secondary">Default</button> 
<footer class="footer footer-disp py-4 px-3">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="row justify-content-between">
      <div class="col-sm-auto">
        <b>Last updated February 2024.</b>
      </div>
    <div class="col-sm-auto text-right">
      <a class="d-inline px-2" href="#">
          Linkedin
      </a>
      <a class="d-inline px-2" href="#">
          Instagram
      </a>
    </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</footer>

Accessibility

Provide a visual focus indicator to allow tabbing through the footer items if links are present. Lastly, make sure the text and background of the footer have sufficient contrast for readability.
